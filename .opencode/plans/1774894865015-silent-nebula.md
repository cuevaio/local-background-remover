# Indie Narrative Reposition Plan

## Goal
Shift the web marketing narrative from team/corporate language to a craft-first indie voice for:
- indie builders
- shippers and indie hackers
- designers and solo/small studios
- buyers who prefer one-time pricing over expensive subscriptions

Use a neutral modern tone (no explicit ideological labels), while preserving product truth: private local processing, app + CLI workflows, public downloads, and license-gated runtime.

- Use **Anthony Cueva** for legal/rights references.
- Keep **cueva.io** as handle/brand reference.
- Keep social links as:

  - Instagram: `https://instagram.com/cueva.io`

## Copy Guardrails
- Prefer: independent, craft-first, modern builders, solo creators, small studios, ship faster, own your workflow.
- Avoid: teams, organizations, enterprise rollout, procurement-heavy language.
- Keep claims practical and verifiable; avoid hype or combative language.
- Keep references to recurring cost/value factual (e.g., one-time pricing, no recurring subscription for core usage).
